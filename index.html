About Us

Welcome to Yahya Gym

Yahya Gym (یحییٰ جِم) is a premium fitness center located in the heart of Garden Town, Lahore. We provide a clean, spacious, and motivating environment for both ladies and gents with completely separate facilities.

Our gym is equipped with modern cardio machines, strength training equipment, and free weights to help you achieve your fitness goals. Our friendly and experienced trainers are always available to guide you.
